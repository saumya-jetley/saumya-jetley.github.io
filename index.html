...the origin story
Given my scientific background, it is not a surprise that I am deeply passionate about AI/tech solution scape and its growing democratization with a rising number of startups. To play my part, a year ago I launched AdhyaAI - an AI/ML consulting tailored to address the punctual 'expertise and strategy' needs of startups.

A startup may not always have the funds to hire broadly or be top heavy, but crucially it needs guidance for its product and data strategy in its formative period.

This is where I come in: working closely with founding teams, where I get to don different hats going from user understanding, market research to product design, co-architecting AI systems that align data and product strategy, building them to learn and scale, defining data governance guidelines, and navigating the solution space of AI models, In short, I love action, challenges and the thrill of working in startups to design and develop robust data and AI stacks adapted to the startup vision and runway.
